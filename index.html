🔒 Runs 100% in your browser — no signup fee, no cloud upload

Turn attendance registers into a full salary sheet — in minutes.

AttendPay reads your attendance PDFs, works out present/absent/leave days per employee, and builds a complete payroll sheet with allowances, deductions and bonuses — all inside your own browser. No account is required to try it, and nothing you upload ever leaves your device.

Open AttendPay → Have a question?
How it works
Step 1

Upload your PDF

Drop in one or more attendance register PDFs — hospital-style registers and generic attendance sheets are both supported.

Step 2

Review the numbers

Present, absent, leave, late and early counts are extracted automatically. Edit any cell directly if something needs correcting.

Step 3

Add pay details

Set base salary, allowances, deductions, overtime and bonus per employee, or in bulk across your whole team.

Step 4

Export & share

Generate individual salary slips, or export the whole sheet to Excel, CSV, or a printable PDF.

Why AttendPay

Private by design

Your attendance data is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server or stored in the cloud.

Free to use

AttendPay is free — no subscription needed to calculate your team's payroll.

Built for real registers

Designed around the messy, real-world formatting of actual attendance PDFs, not a tidy sample spreadsheet.
