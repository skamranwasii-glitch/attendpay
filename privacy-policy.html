Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

AttendPay ("we", "the app") is an attendance-to-salary calculator. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use it, and what information we collect when you visit our website.

Your attendance and payroll data

The core AttendPay tool runs entirely inside your web browser. When you upload an attendance PDF or enter salary details:

  • Files are read and processed locally on your device using JavaScript.
  • Nothing you upload is sent to our servers or any third party.
  • Sign-in details and saved records are stored only in your browser's local storage, on your own device.
  • Clearing your browser data (or using a different device/browser) will remove this locally stored information.

Advertising

AttendPay may display ads served by Google AdSense to support the free version of the tool. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Website analytics

Like most websites, our web pages may collect basic, non-identifying technical information (such as browser type and pages visited) to help us understand how the site is used and to keep it running smoothly.

Contact information you provide

If you contact us directly by email or WhatsApp, we'll use the details you share (such as your email address or message) only to respond to your query, and won't share them with third parties beyond what's needed to do so.

Children's privacy

AttendPay is a business/payroll tool and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
