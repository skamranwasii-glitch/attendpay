AttendPay

AttendPay — Attendance to Salary Sheet

Upload attendance PDFs, auto-extract present/absent/leave per employee, add allowances/deductions/bonus, and generate a full payroll sheet — all inside your browser.

🔒 Runs 100% in your browser — no signup fee, no cloud upload, nothing leaves this device
1UPLOAD PDF(S)

Add attendance PDF

You can upload more than one PDF (e.g. separate sheets for different departments) — results will be combined into one table.

📄
Click to choose PDF(s), or drag and drop here
Multiple .pdf files supported

If your PDF states its own period, it will auto-fill these once processed. You can also set them manually before running — used for the holiday calendar and working-days calculation below.

View raw extracted text (for checking parsing accuracy)
2EMPLOYEE SETUP (OPTIONAL)

Reuse salary & allowance setup

Save your employees' salary/allowance/deduction setup to a file once — next month, import it so you don't have to retype everything, only the attendance PDF changes.

3CALENDAR & HOLIDAYS (OPTIONAL)

Gazetted / public holidays

Add the gazetted holidays that fall inside your period — Sundays are shaded automatically. This calculates Working Days for you (Calendar Days − Sundays − Holidays), which you can apply to the salary calculation below. Holiday dates vary by country and change every year (including moon-sighting holidays), so please add your own rather than relying on a preset list.

4REVIEW & CALCULATE

Payroll Sheet

Click any cell to correct it. Click "+" to open allowances, deductions and bonus for that employee.
